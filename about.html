It's all started by iCoder's Idea.

Hashir founded iCoder to make coding accessible to all skill levels, offering tutorials, coding challenges, and hands-on projects to help users grow from beginners to advanced developers. The platform focuses on practical learning through real-world applications. Community engagement is central to iCoder, where users can collaborate, ask questions, and receive mentorship. Operating on a freemium model, iCoder provides free resources with premium content, such as advanced lessons, job placement assistance, and certifications to support users’ growth and career advancement.

iCoder was founded in 2001 by Hashir, a visionary who overcame significant challenges to establish the company. Determined to succeed, he worked tirelessly, learning new skills, coding late into the nights, and often juggling multiple jobs to fund his dream. Despite the obstacles, Hashir’s unwavering dedication and innovative ideas gradually gained recognition.
